Lovely Dogs

Dogs are loyal, friendly, and very cute animals. They love playing with people and enjoy going for walks. Many dogs are smart and protective. They can make people happy and become wonderful companions for families.

I once have a very cute dog, his name is Moon. He's a small Poodle. For me, he was the first and the best buddy I could ever had. I love him so much. Everyday I came home to a jumping and smilling boy. He was very naughty and playful, he surely loves to run and play with other dogs. I taught him some tricks like "sit", "hands", "down", and he does all the tricks perfectly, he's such a smart boy. But after a year or two, due to my fammily lack of time and caring for him, we have to give him away to a relative's friend. I was heartbroken at that time. Until you have a dog, you will never know what it feels like. But after he got to his new home, just a couple months later, the new owner updated his status for me to know. He was strong and healthy, still naughty and playful but he was so lovely. I was sad, but now not so much, because I know that he is doing well, and he is living a happy life. Love you Moon.
